About Us

This website was created by two KAIST students, Nurbol and Maksim. We noticed that many students struggle with this course, so we decided to develop a platform that provides clear and thorough explanations along with solutions to problems of varying difficulty levels. Our goal is to make learning more accessible and effective. We hope this course will be a valuable resource for students in the future.

Introduction

In this course, you will learn fundamental concepts related to vectors, double integrals, partial derivatives of functions, and more. The course is completely free and accessible to everyone. If you're interested, you can just press buttons bellow, no registration is required.
