QUIZ: Double and Iterated Integrals over Rectangles

Evaluate the integral.

Q1. Evaluate the iterated integral \( \int_{1}^{2} \int_{0}^{4} 2xy \ dy \ dx \)

Solution for Q1

Step 1: Compute the inner integral: \[ \int_{0}^{4} 2xy \ dy = 2x \int_{0}^{4} y \ dy = 2x \cdot \frac{4^2}{2} = 2x \cdot 8 = 16x \] Step 2: Compute the outer integral: \[ \int_{1}^{2} 16x \ dx = 16 \cdot \frac{x^2}{2} \Big|_{1}^{2} = 16 \cdot \left( \frac{4}{2} - \frac{1}{2} \right) = 16 \cdot \frac{3}{2} = 24 \]

Q2. Evaluate the iterated integral \( \int_{0}^{2} \int_{-1}^{1} (x - y) \ dy \ dx \)

Solution for Q2

Step 1: Compute the inner integral: \[ \int_{-1}^{1} (x - y) \ dy = x(y) - \frac{y^2}{2} \Big|_{-1}^{1} \] \[ = x(1 - (-1)) - \frac{1^2 - (-1)^2}{2} = 2x - \frac{1 - 1}{2} = 2x \] Step 2: Compute the outer integral: \[ \int_{0}^{2} 2x \ dx = x^2 \Big|_{0}^{2} = 4 \]

Q3. Evaluate the iterated integral \( \int_{-1}^{0} \int_{-1}^{1} (x + y + 1) \ dx \ dy \)

Solution for Q3

The partial derivatives are:

Step 1: Compute the inner integral: \[ \int_{-1}^{1} (x + y + 1) \ dx = \frac{x^2}{2} + (y + 1)x \Big|_{-1}^{1} \] \[ = \left( \frac{1}{2} + (y+1)(1) \right) - \left( \frac{1}{2} + (y+1)(-1) \right) \] \[ = \frac{1}{2} + y + 1 - \left( \frac{1}{2} - y - 1 \right) = 2y + 2 \] Step 2: Compute the outer integral: \[ \int_{-1}^{0} (2y + 2) dy = y^2 + 2y \Big|_{-1}^{0} = (0 + 0) - (1 - 2) = 1 \]

Q4. Evaluate the iterated integral \( \int_{0}^{1} \int_{0}^{1} \left( 1 - \frac{x^2 + y^2}{2} \right) dx dy \)

Solution for Q4

The partial derivatives are:

Step 1: Compute the inner integral: \[ \int_{0}^{1} \left( 1 - \frac{x^2 + y^2}{2} \right) dx \] \[ = x - \frac{x^3}{6} - \frac{y^2 x}{2} \Big|_{0}^{1} \] \[ = 1 - \frac{1}{6} - \frac{y^2}{2} \] Step 2: Compute the outer integral: \[ \int_{0}^{1} \left( 1 - \frac{1}{6} - \frac{y^2}{2} \right) dy \] \[ = \left( y - \frac{y}{6} - \frac{y^3}{6} \right) \Big|_{0}^{1} \] \[ = \left( 1 - \frac{1}{6} - \frac{1}{6} \right) = \frac{2}{3} \]
