QUIZ: Path Independence, Conservative Fields, and Potential Functions

Evaluate the integrals.

Q1. \( \int_{(0,2,1)}^{(1,\frac{\pi}{2},2)} \left( 2 \cos y \right) dx + \left( \frac{1}{y} - 2x \sin y \right) dy + \left( \frac{1}{z} \right) dz \)

Solution for Q1

Computing each integral separately: \[ \int_0^1 2 \cos y dx = 2 \cos y (1 - 0) = 2 \cos y \] \[ \int_2^{\pi/2} \left( \frac{1}{y} - 2x \sin y \right) dy = \ln(\pi/2) - \ln(2) \] \[ \int_1^2 \frac{1}{z} dz = \ln 2 \]

Q2. \( \int_{(1,1,1)}^{(1,2,3)} 3x^2\,dx + \frac{z^2}{y}\,dy + 2z\ln y\,dz \)

Solution for Q2

Compute the integral for each term separately. \[ \int_1^1 3x^2 dx = \left[ x^3 \right]_1^1 = 1 - 1 = 0 \] \[ \int_1^2 \frac{z^2}{y} dy = z^2 \int_1^2 \frac{dy}{y} = z^2 [\ln y]_1^2 = z^2 \ln 2 \] \[ \int_1^3 2z \ln y dz = 2 \ln y \int_1^3 z dz = 2 \ln y \left[ \frac{z^2}{2} \right]_1^3 = \ln y (9 - 1) = 8 \ln y \]

Q3. \( \int_{(1,2,1)}^{(2,1,1)} \left( 2x \ln y - yz \right) dx + \left( \frac{x^2}{y} - xz \right) dy - xy dz \)

Solution for Q3

The partial derivatives are:

Evaluating each integral: \[ \int_1^2 (2x \ln y - yz) dx = \left[ x^2 \ln y - yzx \right]_1^2 = 3 \ln y - yz \] \[ \int_2^1 \left( \frac{x^2}{y} - xz \right) dy = \left[ x^2 \ln y - xzy\right]_1^2 = x^2 \ln 2 - xz \] \[ \int_1^1 -xy dz = 0 \]
