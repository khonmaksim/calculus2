QUIZ: Triple Integrals in Rectangular Coordinates

Evaluate the integrals.

Q1. \( \int_{0}^{1} \int_{0}^{1} \int_{0}^{1} (x^2 + y^2 + z^2) dz dy dx \)

Solution for Q1

Step 1: Evaluate the inner integral. \[ \int_0^1 (x^2 + y^2 + z^2) dz \;=\; \left[ x^2z + y^2z + \frac{z^3}{3} \right]_0^1 \;=\; x^2 + y^2 + \frac{1}{3} \] Step 2: Evaluate the middle integral. \[ \int_0^1 (x^2 + y^2 + \frac{1}{3}) dy \; = \; x^2y + \frac{y^3}{3} + \frac{y}{3} \bigg|_0^1 \; = \; x^2 + \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{3} = x^2 + \frac{2}{3} \] Step 3: Evaluate the outer integral. \[ \int_0^1 (x^2 + \frac{2}{3}) dx \;=\; \frac{x^3}{3} + \frac{2x}{3} \bigg|_0^1 \;=\; \frac{1}{3} + \frac{2}{3} = 1 \]

Q2. \( \int_{1}^{e} \int_{1}^{e^2} \int_{1}^{e^3} \frac{1}{xyz} dx dy dz \)

Solution for Q2

The partial derivatives are:

Step 1: Evaluate the inner integral. \[ \int_1^{e^3} \frac{1}{xyz} dx \;=\; \frac{1}{yz} \int_1^{e^3} \frac{1}{x} dx \;=\; \frac{3}{yz} \] Step 2: Evaluate the middle integral. \[ \int_1^{e^2} \frac{3}{yz} dy \;=\; \frac{3}{z} \int_1^{e^2} \frac{1}{y} dy \;=\; \frac{6}{z} \] Step 3: Evaluate the outer integral. \[ \int_1^e \frac{6}{z} dz \;=\; 6 \int_1^e \frac{1}{z} dz \;=\; 6 \]
